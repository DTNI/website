About Us

I am what I am because you are

Our investment philosophy derives from the word UNBUNTU, "I am because you are".

Seungsuk (Duke) Lee, CEO of Dt&I, invested in Dt&C(parent company of Dt&I) with a venture fund and successfully got the company listed on the KOSDAQ in 2014.

After a successful IPO, chairman of Dt&C, Charlie Park established Dt&I so that more promising venture companies can be discovered and invested.

Dt&I since its inception continues to work hand-in-hand with venture companies to share market insights, entrepreneurial lessons of seasoned CEOs. All in hopes of ever growing and supporting venture companies leading to the creation of virtuous capital cycle, helping companies help companies.

Dt&I, we strive to be link that connect the venture ecosystem to global ambitions
